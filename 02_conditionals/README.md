# Conditionals

Exercises to practice conditional control statements (if, else if, else, switch).

## Exercises

- **1. Adult Check:** Determines if a person is an adult based on their age (18 or older).
- **2. Max of Three Numbers:** Finds the maximum value among three input integers.
- **3. Leap Year:** Determines if a year is a leap year (divisible by 4 and not by 100, unless divisible by 400).
- **4. Even, Odd, or Zero:** Determines if an integer is even, odd, or zero.
- **5. Grade Letter:** Converts a numerical grade (0-100) to a letter (A, B, C, D, F).
- **6. Basic Calculator:** Performs addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division between two numbers.
- **7. Triangle Classification:** Checks if three lengths form a valid triangle and classifies it (Equilateral, Isosceles, Scalene).
- **8. Zodiac Sign:** Displays the zodiac sign based on the day and month of birth.
- **9. Parking Fee:** Calculates the parking fee based on the hours parked using tiered rates.
- **10. Roman Numerals:** Converts an integer between 1 and 10 to its Roman numeral equivalent.
